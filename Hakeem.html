About Me

Growth and Marketing Executive

Hakeem is presently the Head of Growth and Marketing at Youverify Inc, where He oversees all Marketing and Growth activities of the company. Hakeem has featured in several conferences and seminars including the Gitex Technology week, Dubai 2022. Africa money and de-fi summit, Accra, 2022. Devfest Nairobi, 2022. The Nigerian Fintech week, 2022. Africa NXT, Lagos, 2023. He is a Yali West African RLC Fellow, a YIL fellow, Lagos Business School alumni, 1x TED Speaker, and an MBA candidate at the Quantic school of business and technology. In his free time, Hakeem enjoys volunteering activities. He commits a good amount of time to this as a 2-time board member of Junior chamber international Nigeria, JCI Eko. He also provides career guidance and mentorship to young people across Nigeria through his tech tribe, #GrowwithHakeem He enjoys conversations around social entrepreneurship, technology and Web3
